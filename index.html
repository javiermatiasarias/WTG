Wind Turbine Components

  • Blades

    Description: Long, curved structures that capture the wind.

    Function: Convert wind energy into rotational energy.

  • Nacelle

    Description: The main housing unit on top of the tower.

    Function: Protects internal components like the gearbox, generator, and control systems.

  • Gearbox

    Description: Mechanism inside the nacelle that adjusts the speed of the rotor.

    Function: Increases the rotational speed to a level suitable for electricity generation.

  • Pitch System

    Description: Mechanism at the base of each blade.

    Function: Adjusts the angle of the blades to control wind capture.

  • Cooling System

    Description: Located inside the nacelle, includes fans and radiators.

    Function: Keeps components like the generator and gearbox cool.

  • Yaw System

    Description: Mechanism that turns the nacelle.

    Function: Aligns the rotor with wind direction.

  • SCADA System

    Description: Monitoring and control system.

    Function: Tracks performance and faults, providing real-time data.
