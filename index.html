Wind Turbine Components

  • Blade
    Blades

    Description: Long, curved structures that capture the wind.

    Function: Convert wind energy into rotational energy.

  • Nacelle
    Nacelle

    Description: The main housing unit on top of the tower.

    Function: Protects internal components like the gearbox, generator, and control systems.

  • GBX
    Gearbox

    Description: Mechanism inside the nacelle that adjusts the speed of the rotor.

    Function: Increases the rotational speed to a level suitable for electricity generation.

  • Pitch
    Pitch System

    Description: Mechanism at the base of each blade.

    Function: Adjusts the angle of the blades to control wind capture.

  • Cool
    Cooling System

    Description: Located inside the nacelle, includes fans and radiators.

    Function: Keeps components like the generator and gearbox cool.

  • Yaw
    Yaw System

    Description: Mechanism that turns the nacelle.

    Function: Aligns the rotor with wind direction.

  • SCADA
    SCADA System

    Description: Monitoring and control system.

    Function: Tracks performance and faults, providing real-time data.

  • Trans
    Transformer

    Description: Converts low-voltage electricity generated by the turbine to higher voltage for grid transmission.

    Function: Essential for efficient power transfer to the grid.

  • Vib
    Vibration Sensors

    Description: Sensors placed throughout the nacelle and tower.

    Function: Detects vibrations that could indicate wear or misalignment, helping with early fault detection.

  • Invr
    Inverter

    Description: Converts DC to AC power in some wind turbine models.

    Function: Ensures compatibility with the grid.

  • Lift
    Service Lift or Ladder

    Description: Provides access inside the tower for maintenance personnel.

    Function: Ensures safe maintenance and inspection access.

  • APU
    Auxiliary Power Unit (APU)

    Description: Backup power source in case of grid failure.

    Function: Provides emergency power to essential systems.

  • Fire
    Fire Suppression System

    Description: Fire prevention setup within the nacelle.

    Function: Automatically detects and extinguishes fires in critical components.
